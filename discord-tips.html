Starting out.

In the beginning, the decisions you'll have to make will vary greatly depending on what niche you're server will be in. My only partnered server is a Photography Server, and I made it during a period when there was max 2 Photography-focused servers over 1k members. Needless to say, the niche was mostly untouched.
